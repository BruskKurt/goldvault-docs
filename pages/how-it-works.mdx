# GoldVault features

Find out more about GoldVault's current features below.

# Precious metals prices

GoldVault currently offers SPOT precious metals prices for gold and silver, updated every 5 seconds. These prices are available on the Dashboard home page.

By default, prices are displayed in Euros and Troy Ounces (Oz). You can click on the arrows to the right of the prices (↔) to change the unit of measure (Troy Ounce, Gram and Kilogram). *Your last choice will be saved in the app to load the correct unit of measure the next time *.

To change the currency, go to the app's Settings, in the General → Currency section. You can choose between Euros (default), US Dollar.
